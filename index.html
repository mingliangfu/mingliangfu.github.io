Wadim Kehl

I’m currently chasing the Ph.D. degree in State Key Laboratory of Robotics, Shenyang Institute of Automation (SIA), Chinese Academy of Sciences. My current research mainly focuses on machine learning methods for computer vision, specially deep learning and methods for 3D object pose estimation and tracking. I also have pratical experience in deploying vision algorithms for various robotic applications. Before joining SIA in Sep 2013, I received my BSc degree from School of Physical Engineering at Zhengzhou University in 2013. I spent the first year of MS/PhD program at University of Science and Technology of China to complete course fulfillment.
